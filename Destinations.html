Popular Kenyan Destinations

Explore the best spots for safaris, beaches, and adventures in Kenya.

  • Maasai Mara National Reserve

    The Maasai Mara is world-famous for the Great Migration and the highest concentration of predators in Africa. July to October is peak season.

    Things to Do

    Game drives: Dawn and dusk safaris in 4x4 vehicles.

    Visit Maasai villages: Experience authentic culture and dances.

    Hot air balloon rides: Sunrise flights over the savannah.

    Book Safari

  • Amboseli National Park

    Amboseli offers the most iconic views in Africa — massive elephant herds walking past Mt. Kilimanjaro. Best for photographers.

    Activities

    Elephant tracking: Home to over 1,600 elephants including famous tuskers.

    Photography safaris: Capture Kilimanjaro backdrop shots.

    Observation Hill: Panoramic views of the whole park.

    Book Tour

  • Diani Beach

    Voted Africa’s best beach multiple times. Diani has 17km of white coral sand, coral reefs, and palm trees. Perfect for relaxation after safari.

    Things to Do

    Snorkeling & Diving: Explore coral reefs at Kisite Marine Park.

    Water sports: Kitesurfing, jet skiing, and dhow cruises.

    Colobus Monkey Walks: Visit the Colobus Conservation center.

    Beach Packages

  • Lake Naivasha

    A freshwater lake in the Rift Valley, perfect for birdwatching with over 400 species. Gateway to Hell’s Gate National Park.

    Activities

    Bird watching: Spot fish eagles, pelicans, and flamingos.

    Boat rides: Get close to hippos and giraffes on Crescent Island.

    Hiking Hell’s Gate: Cycle or walk among zebras and gazelles.
