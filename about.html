About TOURCOUR

Your trusted guide to authentic Kenyan adventures

Who We Are

TOURCOUR is your ultimate guide to exploring Kenya's rich culture, breathtaking landscapes, and unforgettable experiences. We aim to inspire travelers to discover the beauty and diversity of Kenya.

Our Story

Founded in 2026, TOURCOUR started with a passion for showcasing Kenya's hidden gems and popular attractions. From the savannahs of Maasai Mara to the beaches of Diani, we curate authentic travel experiences that connect you with the heart of Kenya.

Our Mission

We promote sustainable tourism that benefits local communities and preserves Kenya's natural heritage. Whether you're seeking adventure, culture, or relaxation, TOURCOUR is your trusted companion for responsible travel.

What We Offer

Destination Guides: In-depth info on Kenya's top spots from parks to beaches.

Cultural Insights: Dive into traditions of 40+ ethnic groups and heritage.

Travel Tips: Practical advice for an unforgettable, safe trip.

Meet the Team

Our team includes local experts, travel enthusiasts, and culture lovers committed to showcasing the best of Kenya. All our guides are licensed by the Kenya Professional Safari Guides Association.
